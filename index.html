Gordon's No-Nonsense Kitchen Tips

Listen up, you donuts! Here's how to stop cooking like an absolute disaster:

"A sharp knife is the most important tool in your kitchen. If your knife is dull, you're more likely to cut yourself. Sharpen your knives, for God's sake!"

Season as you go! If you're adding salt at the end, you've already failed. Build layers of flavor throughout the cooking process.

Taste EVERYTHING! How the hell do you know if it's good if you don't taste it?

Keep your station clean! A messy kitchen leads to messy food. Clean as you go, you muppets!

"If you wouldn't serve it to the Queen, don't serve it to your customers. Have some bloody standards!"
